Beacon

Your personal tool for high-value data anchoring and tracking

Privacy Commitment: Beacon is a "local-first" tool. We do not collect, upload, or share any user information or browsing data.

1. Product Overview

Beacon is a minimalist, distraction-free Chrome extension designed for deep reading, research, and long-form study. It allows users to highlight selected text on any webpage and generates corresponding color markers on the right edge of the viewport. By clicking these markers, users can instantly jump back to the exact location of the original text.

2. Data Collection & Usage (Privacy Policy)

We take privacy seriously. Beacon is built on a zero data collection principle:

  • Local Storage: All annotation data (including highlighted text, webpage URLs, and titles) is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage.
  • No Servers: We do not operate any backend servers. Your data never leaves your device.
  • No Tracking: The extension contains no analytics tools, trackers, or third-party advertising scripts.
  • Permissions Disclosure: We request storage permission to save your markers, and scripting and activeTab permissions to render the markers on the page. All operations are performed locally.

3. Key Features

  • Rapid Annotation: Generate markers instantly with shortcuts without breaking your concentration.
  • Intelligent Jump: Smoothly scroll and focus on the original text with a single click on the side marker.
  • Immersive Design: Morandi-toned highlights with low idle transparency and dynamic hover interaction.
  • Management Sidebar: An intuitive interface to manage markers across different domains and clear page data safely.
